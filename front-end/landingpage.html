About Us:

Pegasus connect bridges the educational gap by providing a free and accessible online learning platform for STEM education tailored to the specific needs of children between the ages of 11 and 17 from underserved and under-resourced communities.
Our platform matches students to tutors.

At Pegasus we are reshaping the educational landscape, ensuring that no child is left behind on the journey to a brighter, STEM-enriched future. Our platform goes beyond traditional learning methods, offering personalized STEM modules, interactive tutoring sessions, and a vibrant community that fosters collaboration and support. Mission:

At Pegasus Connect, our mission is to democratize access to quality STEM education for underserved children between the ages of 11 and 17. We are committed to breaking down barriers and fostering an environment where every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, can explore and excel in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our mission is threefold:

Accessibility:

We strive to make STEM education accessible to all by providing an affordable and flexible platform that adapts to the unique needs of underserved communities.

Empowerment:

We aim to empower both students and tutors, fostering a sense of independence and confidence in their STEM abilities. Through personalized learning paths and community engagement, we enable individuals to take control of their educational journey.

Building:

Pegasus is more than just a learning platform; it's a community and a supportive network where students and tutors can connect, share experiences, and inspire one another, creating a positive and inclusive environment for learning and growth.
